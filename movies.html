Shadow Protocol Films

Shadow Protocol I

The original film that started the trilogy. When an experienced agent disappears during a covert operation, the government denies his existence. But the investigation leads to a global conspiracy where the truth is more dangerous than bullets. Secrets come out of the shadows.

Shadow Protocol II

After the conspiracy is exposed, new enemies emerge from within. As the conspiracy deepens and the world is on the brink of chaos, agents are forced to seek allies and form new alliances in the most dangerous places as the danger goes global.

Shadow Protocol: Comeback

The last mission. The past returns with a new threat. The last mission is the most difficult: betrayal, truth and a choice that will decide the fate of the world. The biggest secret has been revealed and it's time to find out who is really behind the protocol. Will Agent X survive the ultimate betrayal?
